About page

Welcome to my audio engineering portfolio! My name is Brandon Mrgich, and I'm passionate about bringing music to life through the art of mixing and mastering. One of the highlights of my portfolio is the recent mastering of my collaborative album, "Ghosts of the Auditorium: Live Performances for an Empty Theatre.” You can listen to “Before & After” version here on the site. I have years of experience doing this for myself, my friends and diverse range of artists and genres, helping them achieve the sound they envision for their music. Whether it's a single, an EP or a full-length album, I approach each project with the goal of making it easy and enjoyable to listen to. Here, you'll also find samples of my work, including snippets from other projects I've worked on. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to the possibility of collaborating with you on your next music project!
